**Outgoing Damage**

- Base values and attribute scaling live under BALANCE.player (src/data/Balance.js (line 2)) and are recomputed whenever the player levels, equips gear, or adds skills (src/entities/Player.js (line 309)). Weapon items and other gear contribute flat dmg plus Might/Will/etc. that further boost stats.dmg (src/data/Items.js (line 2)), while skill cards inject modifiers such as dmgMult (src/data/Skills.js (line 4)). At present only player.stats.dmg is consumed downstream, so the accumulated stats.dmgMult never affects real damage unless you wire it in.
- Every instance of CombatSystem.applyDamage scales the raw attack by BALANCE.combat.playerDamageMult and halves it for buffed enemies via buffedEnemyDamageTakenMult (src/systems/CombatSystem.js (line 24), src/data/Balance.js (line 66)). Anchors toggle isBuffed on nearby foes, so this multiplier matters in those auras (src/entities/Enemy.js (line 371)).
- Core weapon fire originates in Player.processCombat (src/entities/Player.js (line 245)): Hammer swings spawn HammerProjectile, which deals player.stats.dmg * BALANCE.player.hammer.damageMult on each orbit hit (src/entities/Projectile.js (line 98)); Pistol bullets and Staff arcs each use player.stats.dmg * BALANCE.player.[pistol|staff].damageMult and inherit Soul Salvo’s extra shots when salvoCharges are available (src/systems/CombatSystem.js (line 73), src/entities/Player.js (line 465)). Salvo spawns use the same damage calculation but mark isSalvo for visuals/second volley timing (src/systems/CombatSystem.js (line 86)).
- Attribute perks add three more damage sources: Might periodically fires a Shockwave, Alacrity drops Static Mines when moving, and Will summons homing Wisps (src/entities/Player.js (line 410)). Each projectile uses player.stats.dmg multiplied by the appropriate combat scalar (BALANCE.combat.[shockwave|staticMine|wisp]DamageMult) and any projectile-specific multipliers such as the DoT ramp in Static Mines or the final burst multiplier on Wisps (src/systems/CombatSystem.js (line 150), src/entities/Projectile.js (line 296)).
- Phials layer on every other passive damage effect: Ashen Halo ticks for its configurable DPS and radius straight from Phials.ashenHalo (src/entities/Player.js (line 428), src/data/Phials.js (line 2)); Witchglass Aegis retaliates with an AegisPulse that deals pulseBaseDamage + pulseDamagePerStack to everything in its expanding ring (src/entities/Player.js (line 522), src/entities/Projectile.js (line 367)); Soul Salvo is what feeds the extra pistol/staff/hammer shots mentioned earlier (src/entities/Player.js (line 465)); Tithe Engine spends charges to spawn a TitheExplosion that snapshots the configured explosion damage (src/entities/Player.js (line 474), src/entities/Projectile.js (line 1)); and Blinding Step adds a burn DoT by seeding enemy.burns when you dash through targets (src/entities/Player.js (line 497), src/entities/Enemy.js (line 62)).

**Incoming Damage & Mitigation**

- Touching any enemy runs handlePlayerCollision and subtracts that enemy’s configured contact damage each frame or hit: the base class deals 5/10 DPS depending on buffs (src/entities/Enemy.js (line 125)), Walkers override to 10/15 (src/entities/Enemy.js (line 194)), Chargers hit for 15/20 on impact (src/entities/Enemy.js (line 276)), Spitters 3/6 (src/entities/Enemy.js (line 341)), and Anchors 4/8 (src/entities/Enemy.js (line 391)).
- Spitters contribute ranged damage by spawning EnemyProjectile, which adds BALANCE.projectiles.enemy.damage (or buffedDamage) plus the player level before calling takeDamage (src/entities/Projectile.js (line 228), src/entities/Enemy.js (line 324)). Elite Chargers also leave Hazard pools that tick BALANCE.projectiles.hazard.damage * dt whenever the player stands inside (src/entities/Projectile.js (line 345)).
- Boss fights get their own projectile source: each phase fires radial shots that use BALANCE.boss.phase[1|2].projectileDamage on impact (src/entities/Boss.js (line 24)), so the Balance table is the sole place to tweak those values.
- The player cancels all incoming damage while dashing because takeDamage early-outs if dashTimer > 0 (src/entities/Player.js (line 392)). Outside of that window, Witchglass Aegis applies the only damage multiplier on incoming hits by setting aegisDamageMultiplier = 1 - (baseDamageReduction + damageReductionPerStack) for the duration specified in the Phial data (src/entities/Player.js (line 522), src/data/Phials.js (line 20)); the same trigger also causes the retaliatory pulse noted above.
